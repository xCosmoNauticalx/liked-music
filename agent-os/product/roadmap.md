# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP

- Fetch liked songs from YouTube Music
- Convert liked songs to audio files
- Add converted audio files to a local Apple Music playlist
- Track which songs have already been synced to avoid duplicates
- Maintain song order from Youtube Music Liked Playlist in local Apple Music Playlist


## Phase 2: Post-Launch

- Sync playlist order — if the order of liked songs changes in YouTube Music, reflect that change in the Apple Music playlist
