# Product Mission

## Problem

Keeping a local Apple Music playlist in sync with a YouTube Music liked playlist requires tedious manual work. There's no built-in way to bridge these two platforms, especially when the goal is to have actual audio files rather than just catalog matches.

## Target Users

Personal tool — built for the developer's own use, but may be made open source.

## Solution

A local, automated, multi-threaded, one-directional sync tool that fetches liked songs from YouTube Music, converts them to high-quality audio files, and adds them to a local Apple Music playlist. Runs entirely on the user's machine with no external services required.
